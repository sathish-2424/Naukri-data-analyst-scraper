    Frequently asked question

    Q1

    What is the average salary of a Data Analyst in India?

    The average salary of a Data Analyst in India is approximately INR 5.5 lakhs per annum.

    Q2

    What qualifications are required to become a Data Analyst?

    A Data Analyst typically requires a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field.

    Q3

    What skills are required to become a Data Analyst?

    Data Analysts need to have strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as knowledge of database management systems, programming languages, and statistical software.

    Q4

    What is the job description of a Data Analyst?

    A Data Analyst is responsible for collecting, organizing, and analyzing data to identify trends and patterns. They also create reports and present their findings to stakeholders.

    Q5

    What are the duties of a Data Analyst?

    The duties of a Data Analyst include collecting and organizing data, analyzing data to identify trends and patterns, creating reports, and presenting findings to stakeholders.
